What is Vibescape?                                                                                                                      
Vibescape is a collaborative music platform where you create virtual lounges with friends, share your favorite Spotify tracks, vote on songs together, and chat in real-time.
Think of it as your own private DJ room where everyone gets a say in what plays next.
